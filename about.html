About

Nosa Asemota is a Nigerian documentary photographer and storyteller, currently serving as the official photographer to the President of Nigeria. With a background in guidance and counseling, Nosa brings a unique, empathetic approach to his work, capturing authentic narratives that reveal the humanity behind his subjects. As the founder of Lens for Good, an initiative dedicated to empowering young Nigerian creatives, he mentors emerging storytellers to use visual media as a tool for social impact. Nosa's work is driven by a commitment to amplify African voices and bridge cultural divides, with a vision of inspiring change through powerful, relatable storytelling.
